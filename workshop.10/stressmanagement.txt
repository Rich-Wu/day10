1. Create your own action plan for approaching stress

I'll breathe deeply and steadily, close my eyes, remember that everything I'm feeling is fleeting and let the feelings pass through me.

2. Make a list of things that trigger your feelings of stress

Uncertainty, feelings of inadequacy, fear, are things that trigger stress.

3. Make a list of the physical signs you are experiencing stress

Shallow breathing, jittery hands, sweat, lethargy are all signs I'm stressed.

4. Create a list of strategies to use when experiencing stress

Take a break from what it is that's stressing me out and return to it after having rested a bit.

5. Set a Goal and track it!
